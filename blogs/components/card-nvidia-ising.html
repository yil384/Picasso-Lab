Testing NVIDIA Ising: Evaluating Neural Pre-Decoding from Surface Codes to Bivariate Bicycle Codes
We benchmark NVIDIA's CNN pre-decoder on surface codes (up to 2.12x speedup), then test an MLP pre-decoder on bivariate bicycle qLDPC codes — revealing that architecture-code alignment is the key to robust neural pre-decoding.
