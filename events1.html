Congratulations to Zheng Wang on Successfully Defending His PhD Thesis!

We are thrilled to announce that our group member, Zheng Wang, has successfully passed the final defense for his doctoral dissertation titled "Overcoming Input Irregularity: Balanced and Efficient System Designs for Large Scale Model Training".

Dr. Wang has made significant contributions to high-performance system design and optimization for Deep Learning (specifically LLMs and Recommendation Systems) during his time with us. We wish Zheng all the best in his future endeavors!
